Initiate

Draft

STEP 3
Consult

Engage funders, legal, stakeholders · Document all feedback

Roles & Responsibilities
BT
Board of Trustees
Approves Tier 1 & High Strategic policies (quorum ≥50%+1). Fiduciary oversight and King V compliance. Receives all Tier 2 & 3 notifications.
ND
National Director
Approves Tier 2 & High Operational policies. Chairs approval meetings. Cannot delegate Board-level approvals. Commences emergency reviews within 7 or 30 days.
DN
Deputy National Director
Acts in ND's absence when formally delegated in writing with defined scope and duration. Board notified if delegation exceeds 90 days.
RL
Regulatory Watch Lead
Oversees all policy development. Coordinates reviews and version control. Member of PTT. Notifies ND & Chairperson of regulatory changes.
PL
PTT Lead
Custodian of Policy Register. Manages SharePoint & HR Manage. Assigns version numbers and watermarks. Primary contact for all policy inquiries.
PO
Policy Owner
Drafts and updates policy content using the official template. Ensures legal compliance. Oversees training and enforcement. Initiates review cycles.
